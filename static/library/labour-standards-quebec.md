## Overview

The *Act Respecting Labour Standards* (CQLR c N-1.1) establishes minimum working conditions for most employees in Quebec. It is administered by the CNESST.

## Key Topics

- **Wages** — Minimum wage, pay frequency, deductions
- **Hours of Work** — Standard workweek, overtime, rest periods
- **Leaves** — Annual vacation, statutory holidays, family/parental leave, sick leave
- **Termination** — Notice periods, severance, prohibited dismissals
- **Psychological Harassment** — Definition, employer obligations, remedies

## Notable Provisions

**Section 81.18** — Psychological harassment: vexatious behaviour in the form of repeated hostile or unwanted conduct, verbal comments, actions or gestures that affect the employee's dignity or integrity.

**Section 82** — Every employee credited with three months of uninterrupted service is entitled to written notice before termination, varying by length of service.

**Section 124** — An employee with two years of continuous service who believes dismissal was without good and sufficient cause may file a complaint with the CNESST.
