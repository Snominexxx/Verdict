## Overview

The *Cooperatives Act* (CQLR c C-67.2) provides the legal framework for constituting, operating, and dissolving cooperatives in Quebec.

## Cooperative Principles

1. Voluntary and open membership
2. Democratic member control (one member, one vote)
3. Member economic participation
4. Autonomy and independence
5. Education, training, and information
6. Cooperation among cooperatives
7. Concern for community

## Key Provisions

- **Incorporation** — At least five founders; articles filed with the *Registraire des entreprises*.
- **Governance** — Board of directors elected by members; annual general meeting required.
- **Surplus Allocation** — Rebates to members based on patronage; reserves as required.
- **Dissolution** — Assets distributed according to by-laws and this Act.
