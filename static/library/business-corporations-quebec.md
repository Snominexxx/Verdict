## Overview

The *Business Corporations Act* (CQLR c S-31.1) governs the incorporation, organization, and dissolution of business corporations under Quebec law (as opposed to federal CBCA corporations).

## Structure

- **Title I** — Constitution of Business Corporations
- **Title II** — Capital, Shares, and Security Holders
- **Title III** — Directors and Officers
- **Title IV** — By-laws and Resolutions
- **Title V** — Financial Disclosure and Audit
- **Title VI** — Fundamental Changes (amalgamation, continuance, liquidation)
- **Title VII** — Remedies and Enforcement

## Key Concepts

- **Unanimous Shareholder Agreement** — Shareholders may restrict director powers (s. 214).
- **Oppression Remedy** — Shareholders, directors, or creditors may seek relief from oppressive conduct (s. 450).
- **Derivative Action** — A complainant may sue on behalf of the corporation (s. 445).
